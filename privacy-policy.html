Privacy Policy for TabPause

TabPause does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension operates entirely on the client side and does not interact with external servers.

Permissions: The extension requires permissions to interact with browser tabs and local storage solely for its core functionality. These permissions are used exclusively for pausing and resuming video playback when you switch between tabs.
